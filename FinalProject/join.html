Check out our Membership Levels:

Non-Profit - $100

If your business is classified as a non-profit we have a discounted fee. You must be recognized by the governemnt as non-profit and this membership includes all of the bronze member benefits.

Bronze - $200

Bronze members receive a directory listing, a badge to be displayed at business and/or on the business website, and advertising at events sponsored by the Chamber of Commerce.

Silver - $400

Silver members enjoy a directory listing, one free business training each year (with a directory to other recommended trainings), advertising at events sponsored by the Chamber of Commerce, and provided social media posts.

Gold - $1000

The Gold membership includes a directory listing, three free business trainings per year from your choice in our directory, three business luncheons per year, and large advertising at events sponsored by the Chamber of Commerce.
