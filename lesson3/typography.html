H1 font - Dancing Script

Something with a titled like "The Leaf on the Wind" needs a graceful font, something you could believe that a leaf could be swirling through.

H2 font - Poppins Light

For the H2s I chose Poppins, something with a clear contrast to the title- very straight lines, nothing like the swirling font above.

Regular font - Roboto

I chose Roboto for the paragraph font. I love this font for normal text, it's extremely easy to read and it makes a nice contrast with the h1 font.
