Site Name: Leaf on the Wind Weather

Domain Name:

LeafontheWind.com

Site purpose:

A site for broadcasting the weather of (x) community. This particular small-town community is full of nerds who know the reference "I'm a leaf on the wind, watch me fly". "The Leaf on the Wind" also has a poetic ring to it, which appeals to many others in the community who don't necessarily know the reference. The primary objective of the site is to prominently display the weather. It's also a center for the community to showcase upcoming events and photo galleries of past events.
